atha khalu mahāmatir bodhisattvo mahāsattvo bhagavantam ābhiḥ sārūpyābhir gāthābhir abhiṣṭutya
svanāmagotraṃ bhagavate saṃśrāvayati sma -

mahāmatir ahaṃ bhagavan mahāyānagatiṃ gataḥ |aṣṭottaraṃ praśnaśataṃ pṛcchāmi vadatāṃ varam ||
2.9 ||

tasya tadvacanaṃ śrutvā buddho lokavidāṃ varaḥ |nirīkṣya pariṣadaṃ sarvām alapī sugatātmajam
|| 2.10 ||

pṛcchantu māṃ jinasutās tvaṃ ca pṛccha mahāmate |ahaṃ te deśayiṣyāmi pratyātmagatigocaram ||
2.11 ||

atha khalu mahāmatir bodhisattvo mahāsattvo bhagavatā kṛtāvakāśo bhagavataś caraṇayor nipatya
bhagavantaṃ praśnaṃ (24*) paripṛcchati sma -

(11,1) kathaṃ hi śudhyate tarkaḥ kasmāt tarkaḥ pravartate |kathaṃ hi dṛśyate bhrāntiḥ kasmād
bhrāntiḥ pravartate || 2.12 ||

kasmāt kṣetrāṇi nirmāṇā lakṣaṇaṃ tīrthikāś ca ye |nirābhāsaḥ kramaḥ kena jinaputrāś ca te kutaḥ
|| 2.13 ||

muktasya gamanaṃ kutra baddhaḥ kaḥ kena mucyate |dhyāyināṃ viṣayaḥ ko ’sau kathaṃ yānatrayaṃ
bhavet || 2.14 ||

pratyate jāyate kiṃ tatkāryaṃ kiṃ kāraṇaṃ ca kim |ubhayāntakathā kena kathaṃ vā saṃpravartate
|| 2.15 ||

ārūpyā ca samāpattir nirodhaś ca kathaṃ bhavet |saṃjñānirodhaś ca kathaṃ kathaṃ kasmād dhi mucyate
|| 2.16 ||

kriyā pravartate kena gamanaṃ dehadhāriṇām |kathaṃ dṛśyaṃ vibhāvo kathaṃ kathaṃ bhūmiṣu vartate
|| 2.17 ||

nirbhidyet tribhavaṃ ko ’sau kiṃ sthānaṃ kā tanur bhavet |sthitaḥ pravartate kutra jinaputraḥ
kathaṃ bhavet || 2.18 ||

abhijñā labhate kena vaśitāś ca samādhayaḥ |samādhyate kathaṃ cittaṃ brūhi me jinapuṃgava ||
2.19 ||

ālayaṃ ca kathaṃ kasmān manovijñānam eva ca |(20*) kathaṃ pravartate dṛśyaṃ kathaṃ dṛśyān nivartate
|| 2.20 ||

gotrāgotraṃ kathaṃ kena cittamātraṃ bhavet katham |lakṣaṇasya vyavasthānaṃ nairātmyaṃ ca kathaṃ
bhavet || 2.21 ||

kathaṃ na vidyate sattvaḥ saṃvṛtyā deśanā katham |kathaṃ śāśvatocchedadarśanaṃ na pravartate
|| 2.22 ||

kathaṃ hi tīrthikāstvaṃ ca lakṣaṇair na virudhyase |naiyāyikāḥ kathaṃ brūhi bhaviṣyanti anāgate
|| 2.23 ||

śūnyatā ca kathaṃ kena kṣaṇabhaṅgaś ca te katham |kathaṃ pravartate garbhaḥ kathaṃ loko nirīhikaḥ
|| 2.24 ||

māyāsvapnopamaḥ kena kathaṃ gandharvasaṃnibhaḥ |marīcidakacandrābhaḥ kena loko bravīhi me ||
2.25 ||
