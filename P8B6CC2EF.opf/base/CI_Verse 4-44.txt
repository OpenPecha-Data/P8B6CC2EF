saptarātreṇa bhagavān sāgarān makarālayāt |sāgarendrasya bhavanāt samuttīrya taṭe sthitaḥ ||
1.4 ||

sthitamātrasya buddhasya rāvaṇo hy apsaraiḥ saha |yakṣaiś ca nānāvividhaiḥ śukasāraṇapaṇḍitaiḥ
|| 1.5 ||

ṛddhyā gatvā tam adhvānaṃ yatra tiṣṭhati nāyakaḥ |avatīrya pauṣpakād yānād vandya pūjya tathāgatam
|nāma saṃśrāvayaṃs tasmai jinendreṇa adhiṣṭhitaḥ || 1.6 ||

rāvaṇo ’haṃ daśagrīvo rākṣasendra ihāgataḥ |anugṛhṇāhi me laṅkāṃ ye cāsmin puravāsinaḥ || 1.7
||

pūrvair api hi saṃbuddhaiḥ pratyātmagatigocaram |(5*)śikhare ratnakhacite puramadhye prakāśitam
|| 1.8 ||

bhagavān api tatraiva śikhare ratnamaṇḍite |deśetu dharma virajaṃ jinaputraiḥ parīvṛtaḥ |śrotukāmā
vayaṃ cādya ye ca laṅkānivāsinaḥ || 1.9 ||

deśanānayanirmuktaṃ pratyātmagatigocaram |laṅkāvatārasūtraṃ vai pūrvabuddhānuvarṇitam || 1.10
||

smarāmi pūrvakair buddhair jinaputrapuraskṛtaiḥ |sūtram etan nigadyate bhagavān api bhāṣatām
|| 1.11 ||

bhaviṣyanty anāgate kāle buddhā buddhasutāś ca ye |etam eva nayaṃ divyaṃ śikhare ratnabhūṣite
|deśayiṣyanti yakṣāṇām anukampāya nāyakāḥ || 1.12 ||

divyalaṅkāpurīramyāṃ nānāratnair vibhūṣitām |prāgbhāraiḥ śītalaiḥ ramyai ratnajālavitānakaiḥ
|| 1.13 ||

rāgadoṣavinirmuktāḥ pratyātmagaticintakāḥ |santy atra bhagavan yakṣāḥ pūrvabuddhaiḥ kṛtārthinaḥ
|mahāyānanaye śraddhā niviṣṭānyonyayojakāḥ || 1.14 ||

(3,1) yakṣiṇyo yakṣaputrāś ca mahāyānabubhutsavaḥ |āyātu bhagavān śāstā laṅkāmalayaparvatam
|| 1.15 ||

(6*) kumbhakarṇapurogāś ca rākṣasāḥ puravāsinaḥ |śroṣyanti pratyātmagatiṃ mahāyānaparāyaṇāḥ
|| 1.16 ||

kṛtādhikārā buddheṣu kariṣyanty adhunā ca vai |anukampārthaṃ mahyaṃ vai yāhi laṅkāṃ sutaiḥ saha
|| 1.17 ||

gṛham apsaravargāś ca hārāṇi vividhāni ca |ramyāṃ cāśokavanikāṃ pratigṛhṇa mahāmune || 1.18
||

ājñākaro ’haṃ buddhānāṃ ye ca teṣāṃ jinātmajāḥ |nāsti tad yan na deyaṃ me anukampa mahāmune
|| 1.19 ||

tasya tadvacanaṃ śrutvā uvāca tribhaveśvaraḥ |atītair api yakṣendra nāyakai ratnaparvate ||
1.20 ||

pratyātmadharmo nirdiṣṭaḥ tvaṃ caivāpy anukampitaḥ |anāgatāś ca vakṣyanti girau ratnavibhūṣite
|| 1.21 ||

yogināṃ nilayo hy eṣa dṛṣṭadharmavihāriṇām |anukampyo ’si yakṣendra sugatānāṃ mamāpi ca || 1.22
||

adhivāsya bhagavāṃs tūṣṇīṃ śamabuddhyā vyavasthitaḥ |ārūḍhaḥ puṣpake yāne rāvaṇenopanāmite ||
1.23 ||

tatraiva rāvaṇo ’nye ca jinaputrā viśāradāḥ |(7*)apsarair hāsyalāsādyaiḥ pūjyamānāḥ purīṃ gatāḥ
|| 1.24 ||

tatra gatvā purīṃ ramyāṃ punaḥ pūjāṃ pralabdhavān |rāvaṇādyair yakṣavargair yakṣiṇībhiś ca pūjitaḥ
|yakṣaputrair yakṣakanyābhī ratnajālaiś ca pūjitaḥ || 1.25 ||

rāvaṇenāpi buddhasya hārā ratnavibhūṣitāḥ |jinasya jinaputrāṇām uttamāṅgeṣu sthāpitāḥ || 1.26
||

pragṛhya pūjāṃ bhagavān jinaputraiś ca paṇḍitaiḥ |dharmaṃ vibhāvayām āsa pratyātmagatigocaram
|| 1.27 ||

rāvaṇo yakṣavargāś ca saṃpūjya vadatāṃ varam |mahāmatiṃ pūjayanti adhyeṣanti punaḥ punaḥ |tvaṃ
praṣṭā sarvabuddhānāṃ pratyātmagatigocaram || 1.28 ||

(4,1) ahaṃ hi śrotā yakṣāś ca jinaputrāś ca sann iha |adhyeṣayāmi tvāṃ yakṣā jinaputrāś ca paṇḍitāḥ
|| 1.29 ||

vādināṃ tvaṃ mahāvādī yogināṃ yogavāhakaḥ |adhyeṣayāmi tvāṃ bhaktyā nayaṃ pṛccha viśārada ||
1.30 ||

tīrthyadoṣair vinirmuktaṃ pratyekajinaśrāvakaiḥ |pratyātmadharmatāśuddhaṃ buddhabhūmiprabhāvakam
|| 1.31 ||

nirmāya bhagavāṃs tatra śikharān ratnabhūṣitān |anyāni caiva divyāni ratnakoṭīralaṃkṛtāḥ ||
1.32 ||

ekaikasmin girivare ātmabhāvaṃ vidarśayan |tatraiva rāvaṇo yakṣa ekaikasmin vyavasthitaḥ ||
1.33 ||

atra tāḥ parṣadaḥ sarvā ekaikasmin hi dṛśyate |sarvakṣetrāṇi tatraiva ye ca teṣu vināyakāḥ ||
1.34 ||

rākṣasendraś ca tatraiva ye ca laṅkānivāsinaḥ |tatpratispardhinī laṅkā jinena abhinirmitā ||
1.35 ||

anyāś cāśokavanikā vanaśobhāś ca tatra yāḥekaikasmin girau nātho mahāmatipracoditaḥ || 1.36
||

dharmaṃ dideśa yakṣāya pratyātmagatisūcakam |dideśa nikhilaṃ sūtraṃ śatasāhasrikaṃ girau ||
1.37 ||

śāstā ca jinaputrāś ca tatraivāntarhitās tataḥ |adrākṣīd rāvaṇo yakṣa ātmabhāvaṃ gṛhe sthitam
|| 1.38 ||

cinteti kim idaṃ ko ’yaṃ deśitaṃ kena vā śrutam |kiṃ dṛṣṭaṃ kena vā dṛṣṭaṃ nagaro vā kva saugataḥ
|| 1.39 ||

tāni kṣetrāṇi te buddhā ratnaśobhāḥ kva saugatāḥ |svapno ’yam atha vā māyā nagaraṃ gandharvaśabditam
|| 1.40 ||

timiro mṛgatṛṣṇā vā svapno vandhyāprasūyatam |(9*)alātacakradhūmo vā yad ahaṃ dṛṣṭavān iha ||
1.41 ||

atha vā dharmatā hy eṣā dharmāṇāṃ cittagocare |na ca bālā avabudhyante mohitā viśvakalpanaiḥ
|| 1.42 ||

na draṣṭā na ca draṣṭavyaṃ na vācyo nāpi vācakaḥ |anyatra hi vikalpo ’yaṃ buddhadharmākṛtisthitiḥ
|ye paśyanti yathādṛṣṭaṃ na te paśyanti nāyakam || 1.43 ||

(5,1) apravṛttivikalpaś ca yadā buddhaṃ na paśyati |apravṛttibhave buddhaḥ saṃbuddho yadi paśyati
|| 1.44 ||
