madyaṃ māṃsaṃ palāṇḍuṃ na bhakṣayeyaṃ mahāmune |bodhisattvairmahāsattvair bhāṣadbhirjinapuṃgavaiḥ
|| 8.1 ||

anāryajuṣṭadurgandhamakīrtikaram eva ca |kravyādabhojanaṃ māṃsaṃ brūhyabhakṣyaṃ mahāmune ||
8.2 ||

bhakṣyamāṇe ca ye deṣā abhakṣye tu guṇāś ca ye |mahāmate nibodha tvaṃ ye doṣā māṃsabhakṣaṇe
|| 8.3 ||

svājanyādvyabhicārāc ca śukraśoṇitasaṃbhavāt |udvejanīyaṃ bhūtānāṃ yogī māṃsaṃ vivarjayet ||
8.4 ||

māṃsāni ca palāṇḍūṃś ca madyāni vividhāni ca |gṛñjanaṃ laśunaṃ caiva yogī nityaṃ vivarjayet
|| 8.5 ||

mrakṣaṇaṃ varjayettailaṃ śalyaviddheṣu na svapet |chidrāc chidreṣu sattvānāṃ yacca sthānaṃ mahadbhayam
|| 8.6 ||

āhārājjāyate darpaḥ saṃkalpo darpasaṃbhavaḥ |saṃkalpajanito rājastasmād api na bhakṣayet ||
8.7 ||

saṃkalpāj jāyate rāgaś cittaṃ rāgeṇa muhyate |mūḍhasya saṃgatir bhavati jāyate na ca mucyate
|| 8.8 ||

lābhārthaṃ hanyate sattvo māṃsārthaṃ dīyate dhanam |ubhau tau pāpakarmāṇau pacyete rauravādiṣu
|| 8.9 ||

yo ’tikramya munervākyaṃ māṃsaṃ bhakṣati durmatiḥ |lokadvayavināśārthaṃ dīkṣitaḥ śākyaśāsane
|| 8.10 ||
