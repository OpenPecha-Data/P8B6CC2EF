utpādabhaṅgarahito lokaḥ khepuṣpasaṃnibhaḥ |sadasan nopalabdho ’yaṃ prajñayā kṛpayā ca te ||
10.1 ||

śāśvatocchedavarjyaś ca lokaḥ svapnopamaḥ sadā |sadasan nopalabdho ’yaṃ prajñayā kṛpayā ca te
|| 10.2 ||

māyopamāḥ sarvadharmāścittavijñānavarjitāḥ |sadasan nopalabdhāste prajñayā kṛpayā ca te || 10.3
||

dharmapudgalanairātmyaṃ kleśajñeyaṃ ca te sadā |viśuddhamanimittena prajñayā kṛpayā ca te ||
10.4 ||

na nirvāsi na nirvāṇe na nirvāṇaṃ tvayi sthitam |buddhiboddhavyarahitaṃ sadasatpakṣavarjitam
|| 10.5 ||

ye paśyanti muniṃ śāntamevam utpattivarjitam |te bhavantyanupādānā ihāmutra nirañjanāḥ || 10.6
||

mṛgatṛṣṇā yathā grīṣme spandate cittamohanī |mṛgā gṛhṇanti pānīyaṃ vastu tasya na vidyate ||
10.7 ||

evaṃ vijñānabījo ’yaṃ spandate dṛṣṭigocare |bālā gṛhṇanti jāyantaṃ timiraṃ taimirā yathā ||
10.8 ||

dhyātā dhyānaṃ ca dhyeyaṃ ca prahāṇaṃ satyadarśanam |kalpanāmātramevedaṃ yo budhyati sa mucyati
|| 10.9 ||

asārakā ime dharmā manyanāyāḥ samutthitāḥ |sāpyatra manyanā śūnyā yayā śūnyeti manyate || 10.10
||
