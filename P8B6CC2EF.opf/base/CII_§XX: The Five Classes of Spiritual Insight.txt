deśyamāne romāñcitatanur bhavati |lakṣaṇaparicayajñāne
cāsya buddhiḥ praskandati, na pratītyasamutpādāvinirbhāgalakṣaṇaparicaye | idaṃ
mahāmate śrāvakayānābhisamayagotram |yaḥ śrāvakayānābhisamayaṃ dṛṣṭvā ṣaṭpañcamyāṃ bhūmau
paryutthānakleśaprahīṇo vāsanakleśāprahīṇo ’cintyācyutigataḥ samyaksiṃhanādaṃ nadati -

nāsti mokṣo mahāmate |eṣā mahāmate śrāvakayānābhisamayagotrakasyāniryāṇaniryāṇabuddhiḥ
|atra te mahāmate kudṛṣṭivyāvṛttyarthaṃ yogaḥ karaṇīyaḥ ||

(28,1) tatra mahāmate pratyekabuddhayānābhisamayagotrakaḥ, yaḥ pratyekābhisamaye deśyamāne aśruhṛṣṭaromāñcitatanur
bhavati |asaṃsargapratyayād bhāvābhiniveśabahuvividhasvakāyavaicitryarddhivyastayamakaprātihāryadarśane
nirdiśyamāne ’nunīyate,sa pratyekabuddhayānābhisamayagotraka
iti viditvā pratyekabuddhayānābhisamayānurūpā kathā karaṇīyā |etan mahāmate pratyekabuddhayānābhisamayagotrakasya
lakṣaṇam ||
