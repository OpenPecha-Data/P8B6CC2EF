atha khalu mahāmatir bodhisattvo mahāsattvo mahāmatibodhisattvasahitaḥ sarvabuddhakṣetrānucārī
buddhānubhāvena utthāyāsanād ekāṃsam uttarāsaṅgaṃ kṛtvā dakṣiṇaṃ jānumaṇḍalaṃ pṛthivyāṃ
pratiṣṭhāpya yena bhagavāṃs tenāñjaliṃ praṇamya bhagavantaṃ gāthābhir abhyaṣṭāvīt -

utpādabhaṅgarahito lokaḥ khapuṣpasaṃnibhaḥ |sadasan nopalabdhas te prajñayā kṛpayā ca te ||
2.1 ||

māyopamāḥ sarvadharmāḥ cittavijñānavarjitāḥ |sadasan nopalabdhās te prajñayā kṛpayā ca te ||
2.2 ||

śāśvatocchedavarjyaś ca lokaḥ svapnopamaḥ sadā |sadasan nopalabdhas te prajñayā kṛpayā ca te
|| 2.3 ||

māyāsvapnasvabhāvasya dharmakāyasya kaḥ stavaḥ |bhāvānāṃ niḥsvabhāvānāṃ yo ’nutpādaḥ sa saṃbhavaḥ
|| 2.4 ||

indriyārthavisaṃyuktam adṛśyaṃ yasya darśanam |(23*) praśaṃsā yadi vā nindā tasyocyeta kathaṃ
mune || 2.5 ||

dharmapudgalanairātmyaṃ kleśajñeyaṃ ca te sadā |viśuddham ānimittena prajñayā kṛpayā ca te ||
2.6 ||

|| 2.7 ||
